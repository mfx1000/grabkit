FAQ

Does it export the actual post content or just links?

Full content — the complete text body of each post, plus author info, dates, and engagement counts. If a post is truncated with "...see more", the extension expands it before exporting.

Instantly by email after purchase, from Polar (our payment processor). Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within 1 minute.

Does it require me to log into LinkedIn through the extension?

No. It reads your already-logged-in LinkedIn session. No passwords, no OAuth, no special permissions beyond the LinkedIn page itself. Think of it as reading the page the way you would — just automated.

How many saved posts can it export?

All of them. The extension auto-scrolls through your entire saved posts history before exporting. Hundreds of posts? No problem.

Does it send my data anywhere?

No. Everything happens locally in your browser. The exported file downloads directly to your computer. No servers involved. We can't see your posts even if we wanted to. Read our full Privacy Policy.
